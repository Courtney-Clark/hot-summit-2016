Welcome to the landing page for the 2016 HOT Summit! Volunteer mappers, educators, community builders, humanitarians, geographers, and programmers will gather for a multi-track event to learn, discuss and share ideas about the humanitarian and economic development uses of OpenStreetMap. We hope you'll join us.
The planning team is hard at work identifying a venue and setting dates.
Thank you in advance for your patience, and please continue to check back for more details!

Interested in helping plan the 2016 HOT Summit? Email summit@hotosm.org for more information.
